Back To Top Overview Specifications Warranty & Returns Reviews Q&A Overview Specifications Warranty & Returns Reviews Q&A Encircling Beauty and Performance Value-packed with all the essentials, the X550ZA-WH11(WX) reinvents your daily computing experience. It combines high-performance AMD processor and graphics with a splendid display and professional-grade sound quality, making it ideal for work and entertainment. The ergonomic one-piece keyboard and multi-gesture touchpad gives you a precise inputting experience. The ASUS WebStorage lets you easily access your data and files anytime, anywhere. The sleek, ultra-thin exterior is enjoyable to the eye and pleasant to touch. A Classic, Timeless Design Designed for everyday multitasking and entertainment for smart and practical users, the ASUS X Series X550ZA-WH11(WX) notebook redefines your expectations of all-rounder notebook. With a spun-circle finish and patterned palm rests, it adds a touch of sophistication and elegance to everyday computing. Its slim profile is 5% thinner than the previous X Series notebook. The IceCool technology keeps your palm rests cool. Incredible Performance Whether you are using it for work or for fun, the ASUS X550ZA-WB11 is an ideal machine for both productive computing and entertainment. The powerful AMD A10-7400P APU, featuring a speedy quad-core CPU and a premium AMD Radeon R6 GPU on a single die, delivering awesome computing power and fantastic graphic performance. Together with up to 8GB DDR3L memory, it can handle more applications at once without slowdown, resulting in very rewarding usage for work and pleasure. The exclusive Asus “Instant on” resumes your computer from sleep mode in 2 seconds. This means you can get back into fun and productivity delay-free. With instant on functionality enabled, the notebook is able to remain in standby mode for an incredible two weeks. There is no worry about data loss while in standby mode as data automatically gets backed up when the battery drops to 5% charge. Files and tasks are kept safe in memory, waiting for you when you get back. The USB 3.0 ports deliver 10 times faster data transfer speed than USB 2.0. You can save photos, transfer music and open multi-gigabyte video files in a fraction of the time with compatible storage. Lifelike Colors with ASUS Splendid Technology Exclusive Splendid technology delivers vibrant visuals to satisfy the most demanding viewing standards. It fine-tunes display properties based on application, adjusting color gamut and accuracy. The results are more vibrant colors and pristine image quality in all applications, from movies to photos. With a simple shortcut (fn+C), you can turn on Splendid technology and choose the most suitable pre-set based on personal preference and environment. Stunning Sound Quality SonicMaster technology, development efforts of the ASUS Golden Ear team together with ICEpower, offers a remarkably attractive mobile multimedia package. The tailored mix of superior hardware and optimized software improve audio quality with easily distinguishable and accurately louder sounds. Bigger speakers and resonance chambers give a smooth, rich and detailed sound that enhance audio experience in different usage scenarios like movies and music. ASUS SonicMaster technology partnered with the AudioWizard, audio tuning software, adds new depth to your entertainment experience. Multi-touch Made Easy The ASUS Golden Finger team puts natural interfaces first, developing standards far beyond industry average. This assures ASUS provides the most comfortable and responsive experiences in every application and at all times. The ASUS X550ZA-WH11(WX) has a large touchpad equipped with Smart Gesture technology for quick and precise response across a wide range of touch inputs. It delivers naturally simple but intricate interaction, with scrolling and right clicking for access to advanced functions without a mouse. An Ergonomic Seamless One-piece Keyboard The back-assembled, seamless one-piece keyboard prevents key float to provide you with a comfortable and assured typing experience. The ergonomically crafted Chiclet keyboard design spaces out the keys to provide an improved layout that ensures typing accuracy. Each individual key has been expertly tuned to ASUS's Golden Ratio of keyboard force and stroke to increase key responsiveness for faster typing. These details add up to reduce user fatigue even after prolonged periods of typing. Incredible UBIQUITY ASUS WebStorage ensures you get access to your data and files on any device that has an internet connection. With ASUS WebStorage, your files are automatically kept up-to-date and synced across various devices; you can share photos and videos with friends and family, and stream your music and movies to your device. The X550 comes with one year of free 16GB of ASUS WebStorage. Meet the New Windows 10 Windows 10 is so familiar and easy to use, you’ll feel like an expert. The Start Menu is back in an expanded form, plus your pinned apps and favorites so they’re ready and waiting for you. It starts up and resumes fast, has more built-in security to help keep you safe, and is designed to work with software and hardware you already have. Windows 10 comes with Microsoft Edge, an all-new browser that’s built to give you a better web experience. Easily snap up to four apps in place and see all open tasks in a single view. The new Windows Store gives you a unified shopping experience across every Windows 10 device. Cortana works across your day and your devices to help you get things done. By learning more over time, Cortana becomes more personal and useful to you. Windows 10 enables your apps to look and work great in all modes, on all devices.
