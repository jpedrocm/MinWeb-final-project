Back to Top The Samsung NP110S1K 11.6" Notebook M is a compact Windows system that comes with the performance to handle everyday office and home tasks. The system is powered by a 1.6 GHz Intel Celeron N3050 processor that sports two cores and speeds of up to 2.1 GHz. This notebook features 4GB of DDR3L memory as well as a 128GB SSD, providing fast access to your files and apps. Installed on this drive is the Windows 10 Home operating system, which gives you Microsoft's Cortana and Edge web browser. Samsung has equipped the Notebook M with an 11.6" screen that is driven by an Intel HD Graphics engine. This integrated GPU delivers a 1366 x 768 resolution to the screen as well as an external monitor via the notebook's HDMI port. To complete the multimedia experience, a combo audio jack and two 2W speakers are on board. Other features included with the Notebook M are a USB 2.0 port and a USB 3.0 port for connecting with printers, external storage drives, and other USB-equipped devices. Accessing the Internet wirelessly can be accomplished via an 802.11b/g/n Wi-Fi module. Another wireless feature is Bluetooth 4.0, which pairs with speakers and headsets. Aside from wireless connections, the Notebook M comes with a webcam and microSD card reader, which can be used to expand the notebook's storage capacity.
