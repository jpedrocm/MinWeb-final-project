Connected When you're not making the most of the graphics for work, you can use the Macbook Pro to chat via the internet using the integrated webcam or simply surf your favourite sites quickly and easily with the slick Safari web browser. Plus, integrated WiFi connectivity means all you need is a wireless hotspot and the web is yours to enjoy.
