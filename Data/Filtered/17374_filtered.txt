They come with a variety of display sizes and include models that you can use as a traditional laptop or as a touchscreen tablet. Dell products feature the Windows operating system, which offers a familiar user interface and access to thousands of productivity, multimedia and social apps. Windows 8 and Windows 10 also include support for touchscreen keyboards and gesture-based navigation. Staples offers a complete line of these lightweight devices from Dell. Dell Ultrabooks Help You See Data and Media Clearly While these laptops are small, they don't sacrifice display quality. Dell Ultrabook products have 12.5- to 14-inch screens with anti-glare coatings that offer exceptional color quality at resolutions up to Ultra HD 3840 x 2160. Most feature Intel HD graphics processors, which provide plenty of display power for typical applications, but gamers or users with more advanced graphics needs may prefer a device with a dedicated NVIDIA GPU. Many models also have DisplayPort or HDMI connectors that let users attach external monitors or projectors, making them suitable for teachers or presenters. Ultrabook screens give you plenty of room to work with complex spreadsheets, browse websites, watch movies or multitask, and it's all in a very light package that easily fits in a backpack or briefcase. Some models, like the Dell XPS 13 Ultrabook, weigh less than 3 pounds. Choose the Level of Performance You Need Ultrabooks come with a variety of processors from Intel. Efficient i3 CPUs handle everyday computer tasks, such as reading email, word processing or playing music or movies, and they conserve battery power. More powerful i5 processors are ideal for intensive computer tasks such as video games and scientific applications, and the fastest Intel processor, the i7, can efficiently run several heavy-duty programs at once. Most Dell lightweight laptops come with 4GB to 8GB of RAM, plenty of space for most applications. They also have powerful lithium-ion batteries that provide up to 12 hours of battery life on a single charge, depending on the model chosen. Exceptional Data Access Most Dell Ultrabooks come with 128GB to 256GB solid state drives. These devices offer plenty of storage for most users, and computers equipped with solid state drives boot in seconds, making them an ideal choice for frequent travelers or students who often move from place to place. Users who need more space can add external storage using USB data ports or memory card readers. Some ultrabooks also feature Thunderbolt ports, which offer a high-speed data connection for hard drives or other devices. Accessing the Internet with a Dell Ultrabook is easy. They feature built-in Wi-Fi, so you can connect from home, work or the corner coffee shop. These laptops have backlit keyboards and touchpad pointing devices, and they also feature Bluetooth support that simplifies adding additional accessories, such as speakers or mice. They also have built-in webcams, microphones and speakers that support video communications. Ultrabooks from Dell are lightweight devices, but they pack plenty of power into their small cases. Explore the many ultrabooks available at Staples and find the right Dell product to meet any portable computing need.
