For Extreme performance, just slide the GS32 Shadow on the all new redesigned GamingDock Mini. When docked, GamingDock Mini unleashes extreme performance delivering desktop graphical power with additional connectivity. Upgradeable to the latest graphic cards for the ultimate gaming experience. LATEST 6TH GENERATION INTEL CORE i7 PROCESSORS With turbo boost speeds up to 3.1GHz, the 6th gen Intel i7 -6500U processor brings significant improvements to gaming performance, multiple data processing, and 4K video transcoding. This latest CPU architecture paired with faster DDR4 memory is ready to power you through the day. EFFICIENTLY POWERFUL The GS32 Shadow has built-in NVIDIA GeForce 950M powering you with efficient performance through your daily tasks. Enjoy smooth vibrant colors with MSI's exclusive True Color Technology. BOOSTED PERFORMANCE WITH GAMINGDOCK MINI The newly redesigned GamingDock Mini is smaller, lighter and easily upgradeable. When the GS32 is docked, the overall graphics performance shifts to a true desktop experience utilizing the full bandwidth of PCI-e x16. Choose your experience with the dock's ability to fit current and next generation discrete desktop graphics cards. PCI-E GEN3 SSD Harness the full performance potential of the latest PCI-e M.2 SSD through optimization of hardware and software outputting extreme read speeds up to 1600MB/s, 3x faster than SATA3 DDR4 With over 32% faster performance than previous generations, DDR4 is the new standard to take your work and games to the next level. GAME COOL WITH COOLER BOOST 3 MSI's Cooler Boost 3 technology allows for comfortable extended gameplay through intelligently and efficiently designed cooling system. When docked on GamingDock Mini, excess heat is directed out through the back keeping your CPU and GPU stable and cool for hours of gaming. WHITE BACKLIT ERGONOMIC KEYBOARD MSI applied strict quality standards when selecting and designing the ideal keyboard for the GS32 Shadow ensuring a comfortable and responsive typing experience. AN EYE OPENING EXPERIENCE WITH TRUE COLOR TECHNOLOGY See the world for what it is using MSI's exclusive True Color Technology. Precisely calibrated to deliver vibrant colors, stunning details, and sharp images, True Color Technology brings the colors of the world right to your display. Number of external display supported depends on the graphics card you choose to power GamingDock Mini. ESS SABRE HiFi AUDIO DAC - EVOLVE THE WAY YOU HEAR Push the limits of your audio peripherals and hear the difference when you jack into the SABRE Audio DAC. Discover new details that you've been missing or maintain the upper-hand in competitive FPS matches where every bit of sound counts. How we hear is changing. Just like how we see our games has changed over the years with higher resolutions and better graphics. Audio is evolving the same way and we've implemented the next stage of that evolution with SABRE Hi-Fi audio. MILITARY GRADE SURROUND SOUND Technology originally developed for military fighter jet pilots, Nahimic specializes in 3D sound software processing and audio improvements offering virtual 7.1 sound in immersive HD exclusive only in MSI gaming laptops. Protect and prioritize networking traffic while preventing lag spikes so you can game without fear and play at your best. KILLER DOUBLESHOT PRO Don't let lag ruin your experience.
