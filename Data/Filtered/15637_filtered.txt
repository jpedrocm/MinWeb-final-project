Retina display Over 5 million pixels make up the unique 15.4” Retina display for a remarkably detailed and vivid picture – ideal for image retouching or watching HD movies. Even text is razor sharp, making everyday tasks such as web browsing and document editing easier than ever. Thanks to IPS technology, this MacBook Pro offers a wide viewing angle with reduced glare and formidable colours. The 2800 x 1800 resolution is complimented by Intel's Iris Pro Graphics to deliver brilliantly fluid images. OS X Yosemite The Mac Pro runs on Apple's intuitive Yosemite operating system. Simple to navigate yet capable of professional-level computing, it provides an expressive platform for you to work and play the way you want. iCloud is integrated into every aspect of Yosemite, so you can share documents between all your Apple products and enjoy the peace of mind that comes with automatic backup and Time Machine compatibility.  There are over 200 new features and updates compared to the previous generation OS, including improved software and device integration, new apps and enhancements for users with multiple displays. High speed storage With a generous 256 GB of solid state storage aided by 16 GB of RAM, booting, loading and saving is fast and smooth. In addition to smooth working, 256 GB is ideal for storing documents and multimedia files. The MacBook Pro is simple to navigate thanks to the Force Touch trackpad which offers a responsive, interactive and uniform click no matter where you press. Thunderbolt This impressive laptop also sports two of Apple's innovative, blisteringly fast Thunderbolt 2 ports, which offers input/output speeds which, at up to 10 Gbps, are more than 20 times faster than USB 2.0 and 12 times faster than FireWire 800. It can deliver support for video and eight-channel audio and easily hook up with HDMI-compatible devices via an adapter. Thanks to AC wireless connectivity you can enjoy fast, responsive browsing, webcam chat and streaming, while Bluetooth technology makes it easy to connect external devices. For work or play, the Apple MacBook Pro 15" with Retina Display MJLQ2B/A offers a stunning, sleek look and feel with a remarkable battery life of up to 9 hours. Order online and collect in store Sometimes you’ll want something even quicker than we can deliver. Or you can’t be in when our van or courier would arrive. That’s why we offer you the option to order online and collect in store. Subject to what you’re ordering and where you want to collect from, you can: Reserve & Collect – reserve online for free and collect at a local store as soon as you like. Once reserved, your item’s held till closing time the next day, to be paid for when you collect. Pay & Collect – pay online, and have the item sent, for free, from our warehouse to a local store for you to collect. Starting on the date the item arrives in store – which you choose in checkout – you have 28 days to collect it.
