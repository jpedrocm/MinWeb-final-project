They feature powerful CPUs paired with dedicated graphics cards, ensuring smooth performance, and they have plenty of storage space for music, movies and, of course, games. Staples carries a range of Dell products suitable for casual or hardcore gamers. Exceptional Gaming Power Featuring powerful processors such as the Intel i5 and i7, gaming laptops from Dell offer excellent performance for heavy-duty game sessions or serious number crunching. Multicore CPUs and 4GB to 32GB of high-speed DDR3 or DDR4 RAM ensure users can switch seamlessly between applications while they leave their favorite game running in the background. These products come with Microsoft Windows, which provides access to thousands of familiar applications and games. Beautiful Graphics Performance These products have dedicated graphics processors from Nvidia paired with high-resolution LCD displays that create a beautiful and engaging visual experience. They include ultra-portable devices with 13-inch screens and powerful desktop replacements with 17-inch displays. Most models offer full HD 1920 x 1080 resolution that's ideal for playing games or watching movies, and select products have 3840 x 2160 UHD screens. Some gaming laptops from Dell also have touchscreens that further enhance interactivity. Store Games, Music and More Video games consume plenty of disk space, so almost all gaming laptops from Dell come with 1TB or larger hard drives. That's enough room for an extensive gaming library, plus a music and movie collection and personal data. Many laptops also have solid state drives. These devices offer exceptionally short boot times and fast access to critical files. Stay Connected Gaming products from Dell make it easy to get online anywhere. Use the built-in Wi-Fi for casual web browsing and streaming videos, or switch to the Ethernet port for intense games that require a low-latency connection. Multiple USB 3.0 ports let users add peripherals such as game controllers or external drives, and HDMI ports provide a convenient link to a second monitor or television to share the gaming action with friends. Dell Alienware products have Thunderbolt ports that offer additional high-speed data connection options as well as an Alienware graphics amplifier port. This technology lets users add a full-size graphics card to an Alienware laptop, further enhancing the gaming experience. Take Your Dell Gaming Laptop Anywhere Laptops for gaming from Dell use multi-cell lithium-ion batteries that can handle long gaming sessions. They offer up to 11 hours of battery life on a single charge. These products have advanced built-in cooling systems, ensuring reliable performance anywhere. Gaming products from Dell are also light, so it's easy to play your favorite games almost anywhere. Most weigh 6 pounds or less. Once you've reached your destination, keep your gaming gear secure with the built-in lock slot featured on these products. Video games are demanding applications, and Dell gaming products are designed to meet the challenge. They feature advanced processors, dedicated graphics cards and plenty of storage, and they include data ports and networking options that keep users connected anywhere. Explore the Dell laptops available at Staples and elevate your game.
