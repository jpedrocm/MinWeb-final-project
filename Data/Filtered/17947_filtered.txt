For daily computer needs, a notebook provides home and business users, students and gamers dependable performance at a reasonable price. Staples carries a wide range of laptops from manufacturers like Samsung to fit any budget and lifestyle. Super Thin and Lightweight Many laptops from Samsung are extremely lightweight, with some models weighing less than two pounds. They are also exceptionally thin, with many models measuring less than half an inch. The size and weight make these laptops easy to slide into a backpack or notebook bag and carry around anywhere. Your Choice of Operating Systems and Productivity Apps Samsung laptops are available with the Windows operating system and are compatible with Microsoft Office. However, if users prefer something different, Samsung Chromebooks offer Google's Chrome operating system and a selection of Google products such as Gmail, Hangouts, YouTube and Google Search. Most models also include Samsung's SideSync, a proprietary technology that lets users respond to texts, answer calls and get phone notifications on the laptop screen in real time. With SideSync users can also instantly drag and drop files between a compatible smartphone and the laptop. Fast Processing and Easy Multitasking Every Samsung laptop is equipped with either a Samsung Exynos or an Intel multi-core processor that's both fast and efficient so users can simultaneously run multiple programs. Many models also include solid-state hard drives, which have no moving parts and are more durable than traditional HDD's. System memory ranges from 2 to 4GB on Chromebooks to up to 8GB on notebooks, allowing for smooth operation and dynamic multitasking. High-Quality Displays and Powerful Components Some models feature full high-definition, anti-reflective widescreen displays with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels. Others are equipped with WQXGA displays with a resolution of 2560 x 1600 pixels and an Outdoor Mode that ensures the screen stays bright in any conditions. Both displays provide crisp, clear images in a wide range of environments. Some also include an anti-glare coating and LED backlight for optimal viewing in low-light conditions. Long-Lasting Batteries With Less Charging Times All laptops and Chromebooks from Samsung feature powerful batteries that can last from 8 to 12 hours on a single charge, allowing users to continue working, listening to music or watching streaming media on entire flights or road trips, or spend a full day working without having to stop and charge the device. Many models also feature Fast Charging and can recharge from a dead battery to full in about 90 minutes. Multiple Connectivity Options These laptops offer both Wi-Fi and Bluetooth for wireless access to the internet. With built-in USB and HDMI ports, it's easy to connect peripheral devices such as a mouse, external hard drive or monitor. When a Samsung Laptop Won't Do In some cases, a laptop may be a little too much, and a regular tablet may not be enough. Fortunately, Samsung also makes large, 18.4-inch Android tablets that offer plenty of RAM and internal storage as well as slots for external memory cards.
