Back To Top Overview Specifications Warranty & Returns Reviews Q&A Overview Specifications Warranty & Returns Reviews Q&A MSI C-series CX72 Laptop LATEST 6TH GENERATION INTEL CORE i7 PROCESSORS The new 6th generation Intel processors require lower power consumption but deliver better performance than before. CPU performance of Core i7-6700HQ is 20% faster than i7-4720HQ under average usage. PCI-E GEN3 x4 SSD – Ultra Speed Storage The latest PCI-E Gen 3.0 x4 bandwidth utilizing NVMe technology on single M.2 SSD harnesses the full performance potential of the Gen 3.0 SSD through the optimization of the hardware and software, outputting extreme read speeds up to 2200MB/s, 5x faster than SATA3 SSD's. LATEST ARCHITECTURE DDR4- 2133 MEMORY SUPPORT Get the best possible performance out of your notebook with support of the advanced DDR4-2133 memory, achieving over 29GB/s in reading speed and 32GB/s in writing. With over 30% faster performance than previous generations DDR3-1600, DDR4-2133 is the new laptop standard to take your computing experience to the next level. FASTER, SMALLER AND MORE CONVENIENT USB3.0 TYPE C INTERFACE C series supports 3.0 generation USB standard for notebooks and electronic devices. The new SuperSpeed transfer mode supports transferring data up to 5Gbps, around 10 times faster than the previous USB 2.0 standard. The type C usb port is smaller than the old type A port and Its reversible design makes it more convenient than before. "SHIFT" - BOOST PERFORMANCE UNDER CONTROLLED NOISE & TEMPERATURE The exclusive MSI "SHIFT" technology pushes system to the extreme while minimizing noise and temperature. Default setting for the C Series is at SPORT mode. Switch between two other profiles "Green" and "Comfort" to reach balance between system performance and comfortable user experience. * SHIFT is activated by the FN+F7 hotkey combo GREEN Enables lowest power consumption of both CPU and GPU while maintaining coolest constant temperatures for both COMFORT Enables enough power consumption of the GPU while maintaining comfortable temperatures of the GPU SPORT Enables max power consumption of both CPU and GPU for extreme performance EXCLUSIVE COOLER BOOST TECHNOLOGY MSI's exclusive Cooler Boost Technology provides powerful cooling design by boosting the airflow, keeping notebook and chipsets temperature 5%~10% lower. No matter how intense your game or work gets, the Cooler Boost technology always keeps the system cool even after long sessions of extreme use. INSANELY POWERFUL. NVIDIA GEFOCE 940MX DISCRETE GRAPHICS CARD WITH DDR3 2GB VRAM The NVIDIA Geforce 940MX Series brings next-generation graphics performance to gaming notebooks, delivering fast and fluid gameplay experience at the highest settings and resolutions in a lightweight and portable design. Scoring P2,800 in 3DMark 11, the NVIDIA Geforce 940MX shows a 14% graphics performance enhancement compared to the P2450 of GeForce 940M. MATRIX DISPLAY UP TO 2 EXTERNAL MONITORS MSI exclusive Matrix Display Technology delivers up to three monitors in total (built-in x 1 + external x 2), supporting multi-task when needed. BETTER VISUAL & AUDIO EXPERIENCE HDMI delivers uncompressed HD video and multi-channel audio in a single cable, supporting all HD formats including 720p, 1080i, and 1080p. POWERFUL AUDIO-VIDEO INTERFACE MSI C series notebook is equipped with Mini-DisplayPort 1.2. Supporting a 4K (4096x2160@60Hz) display output is therefore made possible. SUPPORT 4K OUTPUT On MSI C series, both HDMI and Mini-DisplayPort are ideal solutions for 4K (4096x2160) output. Simply select the one that suits you the best, and then you are good to go! COMMUNICATE WITH EASE HD Webcam (30fps@720p) permits you to easily communicate with friends and families or even colleagues when at work. The high quality webcam delivers clear and high resolution videos and crystal clear voice recording. PREMIUM SOUND TECHNOLOGY (AUDIO BOOST WITH 4 SPEAKERS) Digital audio signal is repaired and enhanced through the special circuitry design. The audio signal will then be transformed into actual audio sound through the high quality gold plated audio jack which ensures stable sound transmission and reduces noise and sound distortion, delivering Hi-Fi quality audio output. ERGONOMIC KEYBOARD MSI applies strict quality standards when selecting the ideal keyboard for C series notebook. Therefore, a comfortable and responsive typing experience is ensured.
