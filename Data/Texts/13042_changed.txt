Back To Top Overview Specifications Warranty & Returns Reviews Q&A Overview Specifications Warranty & Returns Reviews Q&A The Acer Aspire E5-553G-1986 is a great everyday laptop that delivers performance, features and design at a great price. Designed and optimized for multimedia and gaming, it packs AMD quad-core processor, generous amount of memory, and 15.6" Full HD display driven by an AMD graphics card. The latest connectivity options are included, like USB 3.1 Type C, HDMI, 802.11ac Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. You'll also appreciate the dedicated numeric keypad that crunch number entry, and HD webcam & digital microphone that gear you up for crystal clear video chats without echoes or buzzing. This 15.6" laptop makes everyday better, keeping you entertained or productive whether at home or on the go. Superb Performance Equipped with AMD A12-9700P quad-core processor and mainstream AMD Radeon R8 M445DX graphics card, this laptop offers adequate performance to breeze through your everyday tasks, like web browsing, office documents, online video, social networking and casual games. 15.6" Full HD Display 1920x1080 resolution in a 16:9 aspect ratio makes movies more real and dramatic. Small details and text are clear. Sensational Effects With added enhancements, you get an even richer media experience. Acer TrueHarmony delivers richer, more lifelike audio, while Acer ComfyView reduce eyestrain during extended viewing or reading. USB 3.1 Type C Port The USB 3.1 Type C connector features reversible design for proper connection every time, eliminating frustration and possible port damage. With up to 5Gpbs bandwidth, USB 3.1 (gen 1) is ten times faster than USB 2.0. You can save photos, transfer music and open multi-gigabyte video files in no time with compatible storage. Wireless 802.11ac up to 3X Faster Enjoy wireless data transfer rates of up to three times faster than those of 802.11n. Bluetooth Support The Bluetooth connectivity makes it easy to pair with compatible devices for easy sharing and syncing. Precision Touchpad More responsive than traditional touchpads, these also feature Palm Rejection tech that ignores unintended motions. Numeric Keypad Enter numbers quickly and easily, and with fewer input errors.
