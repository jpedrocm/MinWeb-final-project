"SHIFT" - BOOST PERFORMANCE UNDER CONTROLLED NOISE & TEMPERATURE The exclusive MSI "SHIFT" technology pushes system to the extreme while minimizing noise and temperature. Default setting for the C Series is at SPORT mode. Switch between two other profiles "Green" and "Comfort" to reach balance between system performance and comfortable user experience. Green Disables Intel Turbo boost and limits GPU frequency to level 2. Control CPU and GPU temperatures under 85? Comfort Limits GPU frequency to leve 1. Maintain GPU temperature under 89? Sport Runs CPU and GPU at the highest frequency for maximum performance. EXCLUSIVE COOLER BOOST TECHNOLOGY MSI's exclusive Cooler Boost Technology provides powerful cooling design by boosting the airflow, keeping notebook and chipsets temperature 5%~10% lower. No matter how intense your game or work gets, the Cooler Boost technology always keeps the system cool even after long sessions of extreme use. NVIDIA GEFORCE TECHNOLOGY The NVIDIA Geforce 940M Series brings extreme graphics performance to, providing fast and fluid GPU computing power in a lightweight and portable design. MATRIX DISPLAY UP TO 2 EXTERNAL MONITORS MSI exclusive Matrix Display Technology delivers up to three monitors in total (built-in x 1 + external x 2), supporting multi-task when needed. BETTER VISUAL & AUDIO EXPERIENCE HDMI delivers uncompressed HD video and multi-channel audio in a single cable, supporting all HD formats including 720p, 1080i, and 1080p. POWERFUL AUDIO-VIDEO INTERFACE MSI C series notebook is equipped with Mini-DisplayPort 1.2, supporting a 4K display output SUPPORT 4K OUTPUT On MSI C series, both HDMI and Mini-DisplayPort are ideal solutions for 4K (4096x2160) output. PREMIUM SOUND TECHNOLOGY Enjoy sound through the high quality gold plated audio jack which ensures stable sound transmission and reduces noise and sound distortion, delivering Hi-Fi quality audio output. ERGONOMIC KEYBOARD MSI applies strict quality standards when selecting the ideal keyboard for C series notebook. Therefore, a comfortable and responsive typing experience is ensured.
