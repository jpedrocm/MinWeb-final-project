The Very Modest Price. Made for the Cloud Get the new Acer Aspire One Cloudbook11 – a cloud-minded Windows laptop, and reinvent your mobile computing experience. It combines powerful Intel processor and graphics, super-fast wireless connection, Microsoft’s OneDrive cloud storage, and a host of other features – including a brilliant 11.6" ComfyView HD display, powerful speakers, and multiple connectivity options (one USB 3.0 port/one HDMI port). It keeps your productivity in and out of the office. Speed-boosted Wireless Networking With your head in the cloud, you have a need for speed. The Cloudbook 11 has the latest 802.11ac wireless networking that supports faster-than-ever connection speeds. It makes your cloud storage speedy, your apps peppy and gives your browsing a big boost. Thin, and Really Light Packing every feature you need into a beautiful, clean and simple design, the Cloudbook 11 is just 0.7” thin. And at only 2.54 pounds, this flyweight laptop not only fits perfectly in your bag, it won’t drag you down either, wherever you go. Powerful Inner Strength Confidently take on your day-to-day tasks. Powered by Intel Celeron N3050 dual-core processor (1.6 GHz), 2 GB of RAM, 16 GB SSD and premium integrated Intel HD graphics, the Cloudbook 11 delivers massive power to handle your daily tasks with speed and smoothness. Web pages, games and apps fire up fast and run smoothly. Ready for Cortana Cloudbook 11 gives you a full-fledged Windows 10 pro experience. That means you get the new Cortana personal digital assistant with voice commands that use the built-in dual mics to help you get things done, hands-free. Office & OneDrive Included For a double dose of productivity, the Cloudbook 11 comes with a personal 1-year subscription to the full version of Microsoft Office, plus a place to put all your stuff – 1 TB of accessible-from-anywhere online storage with Microsoft OneDrive. Thanks to the Build Your Own Cloud technology, you can set up additional private cloud storage. Bright, Clear and Comfortable Enjoy greater immersion in your videos, games, and everyday tasks. The stunning 11.6" LED-backlit display features a high resolution of 1366 x 768 to provide incredibly clear, bright images with vivid colors. Moreover, it’s designed with eye-care in mind. The exclusive Acer ComfyView innovations reduce annoying glare, delivering most-comfortable viewing. Clear as a Bell The Cloudbook 11 doesn’t forget about audio quality either. Its dual digital microphones produce clear online calls, so they’ll hear you loud and clear, from anywhere. And of course its powerful speakers provide more realistic and lifelike sound reproduction. The Very Modest Price. Made for the Cloud Get the new Acer Aspire One Cloudbook11 – a cloud-minded Windows laptop, and reinvent your mobile computing experience. It combines powerful Intel processor and graphics, super-fast wireless connection, Microsoft’s OneDrive cloud storage, and a host of other features – including a brilliant 11.6" ComfyView HD display, powerful speakers, and multiple connectivity options (one USB 3.0 port/one HDMI port). It keeps your productivity in and out of the office. Speed-boosted Wireless Networking With your head in the cloud, you have a need for speed. The Cloudbook 11 has the latest 802.11ac wireless networking that supports faster-than-ever connection speeds. It makes your cloud storage speedy, your apps peppy and gives your browsing a big boost. Thin, and Really Light Packing every feature you need into a beautiful, clean and simple design, the Cloudbook 11 is just 0.7” thin. And at only 2.54 pounds, this flyweight laptop not only fits perfectly in your bag, it won’t drag you down either, wherever you go. Powerful Inner Strength Confidently take on your day-to-day tasks. Powered by Intel Celeron N3050 dual-core processor (1.6 GHz), 2 GB of RAM, 16 GB SSD and premium integrated Intel HD graphics, the Cloudbook 11 delivers massive power to handle your daily tasks with speed and smoothness. Web pages, games and apps fire up fast and run smoothly. Ready for Cortana Cloudbook 11 gives you a full-fledged Windows 10 pro experience. That means you get the new Cortana personal digital assistant with voice commands that use the built-in dual mics to help you get things done, hands-free. Office & OneDrive Included For a double dose of productivity, the Cloudbook 11 comes with a personal 1-year subscription to the full version of Microsoft Office, plus a place to put all your stuff – 1 TB of accessible-from-anywhere online storage with Microsoft OneDrive. Thanks to the Build Your Own Cloud technology, you can set up additional private cloud storage. Bright, Clear and Comfortable Enjoy greater immersion in your videos, games, and everyday tasks. The stunning 11.6" LED-backlit display features a high resolution of 1366 x 768 to provide incredibly clear, bright images with vivid colors. Moreover, it’s designed with eye-care in mind. The exclusive Acer ComfyView innovations reduce annoying glare, delivering most-comfortable viewing. Clear as a Bell The Cloudbook 11 doesn’t forget about audio quality either. Its dual digital microphones produce clear online calls, so they’ll hear you loud and clear, from anywhere. And of course its powerful speakers provide more realistic and lifelike sound reproduction.
