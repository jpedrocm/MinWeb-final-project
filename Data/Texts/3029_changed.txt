Back to Top Travel light and stay connected with the 13.3" ZenBook Flip UX360CA Series Multi-Touch 2-in-1 Notebook from Asus. This notebook has a 360 rotatable display so you can use it in laptop mode or tablet mode. Furthermore, it's thin, lightweight, and has a long-lasting battery for all-day use. Powered by a 900 MHz Intel Core m3-6Y30 Dual-Core processor and 8GB of 1866 MHz LPDDR3 RAM, the ZenBook Flip lets you efficiently multitask and quickly access frequently used files and programs. When you need more processing power, the CPU's core clock can reach up to 2.2 GHz with Intel Turbo Boost technology. The 13.3" glossy touchscreen has a 1920 x 1080 Full HD resolution for crisp and clear visual content. Additionally, since it's built with an In-Plane-Switching (IPS) panel, you'll benefit from wide viewing angles and vivid colors. With touchscreen functionality, you'll be able to utilize touch commands and gestures in Windows 10 to further enhance productivity and accessibility. Driven by integrated Intel HD graphics, the ZenBook Flip can handle everyday multimedia needs, such as watching videos, as well as light gaming. You can even connect an external monitor using its micro-HDMI port for enhanced multitasking capabilities. For storage, the ZenBook Flip packs a 512GB SATA III M.2 SSD. SSDs are constructed without any moving parts, so they can endure more and perform faster than traditional hard drives. Should you need more room, you can connect external storage devices via its USB ports and its microSDXC card reader, which can also be used for quickly transferring data from your mobile devices. Connect to the Internet via 802.11ac Wi-Fi and pair wireless devices with Bluetooth 4.1 technology. Other integrated features includes speakers, microphones, a webcam, and a 3.5mm microphone and headphone combo jack. This notebook has a 360 rotatable display so you can use it in laptop mode or tablet mode. Furthermore, it's thin, lightweight, and has a long-lasting battery for all-day use. Powered by a 900 MHz Intel Core m3-6Y30 Dual-Core processor and 8GB of 1866 MHz LPDDR3 RAM, the ZenBook Flip lets you efficiently multitask and quickly access frequently used files and programs. When you need more processing power, the CPU's core clock can reach up to 2.2 GHz with Intel Turbo Boost technology. The 13.3" glossy touchscreen has a 1920 x 1080 Full HD resolution for crisp and clear visual content. Additionally, since it's built with an In-Plane-Switching (IPS) panel, you'll benefit from wide viewing angles and vivid colors. With touchscreen functionality, you'll be able to utilize touch commands and gestures in Windows 10 to further enhance productivity and accessibility. Driven by integrated Intel HD graphics, the ZenBook Flip can handle everyday multimedia needs, such as watching videos, as well as light gaming. You can even connect an external monitor using its micro-HDMI port for enhanced multitasking capabilities. For storage, the ZenBook Flip packs a 512GB SATA III M.2 SSD. SSDs are constructed without any moving parts, so they can endure more and perform faster than traditional hard drives. Should you need more room, you can connect external storage devices via its USB ports and its microSDXC card reader, which can also be used for quickly transferring data from your mobile devices. Connect to the Internet via 802.11ac Wi-Fi and pair wireless devices with Bluetooth 4.1 technology. Other integrated features includes speakers, microphones, a webcam, and a 3.5mm microphone and headphone combo jack.
