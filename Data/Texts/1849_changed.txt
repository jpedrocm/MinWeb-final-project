Exclusive enhanced palm rejection technology improves accuracy and responsiveness for smoother input. The built-in fingerprint sensor provides instantly and securely logging into Windows. Instantly Accessible, Totally Secure The ASUS Q504 features two-second Instant On resume from sleep. This feature lets you get straight back to work or enjoyment where you left off, and with no need to worry about losing data while in standby mode for up to 14 days — your data is automatically backed up when the battery drops to 5% charge. Files and tasks are kept safe in memory, waiting for your return. Advanced Battery Technology for Three Times Longer Life The ASUS Q504's prismatic power packs a great improvement over conventional battery technology. The battery can be recharged up three times more than conventional batteries ordinary batteries, giving you longer life. Enjoy enhanced technology, greener computing, and extended savings. USB 3.0 is Ten Times Faster! The Q504 has 4 USB 3.0 ports (including a USB-C port) for data transfers that are up to ten times faster than a USB 2.0 port. Imagine moving a 50GB file — the equivalent of a Blu-ray movie — in less than two minutes! USB 3.0 isn't just quicker, it's also more reliable, stable, and can provide more power to devices. USB 3.0 is backwards-compatible with all your existing USB 2.0 devices.
