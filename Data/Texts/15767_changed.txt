Product information Specification Delivery, Order & Collect and Services Top deals 8 Product information For a compact and affordable laptop which is perfect for life on the go, try the Asus E200HA 11.6" Laptop in white. Social The Asus E200HA is part of our Social range, which features great-value laptops designed to keep you connected to what matters. It's ideal for social media, making notes, email, and shopping online. Mobile performance The E200HA weighs less than 1 kg and has a compact, space-saving design. With an 11.6" screen, it is designed to be easily portable to allow you to use it wherever you are for everyday computing-on-the-go. Even the power adapter has been designed to be easily portable, measuring just 53 mm x 53 mm. With up to 12 hours of battery life on full charge, you can be confident of using the laptop any time, anywhere. The  E200HA benefits from a quad-core Intel® Atom™ Processor x5-Z8300. Two additional cores result in impressively fast multi-tasking in comparison to laptops with dual-core processors. Premium feel Whilst being a value notebook, the E200HA features all of the essentials you could need with streamlined curves and a tactile finish with a bold white colour. Two high quality front-facing speakers situated under the palm rest direct sound towards you, fully immersing you in your favourite movies and music. With a 1 year subscription to Microsoft Office 365 Personal, you can access full versions of all your favourite programs such as Word, Excel, Outlook, Powerpoint and Access. This helps to further enhance the practical nature of the E200HA, allowing you to work wherever you are. AC WiFi AC WiFi can deliver up to three times faster network speeds when connected to an AC rated router, so you can enjoy higher quality streaming, smoother browsing and faster downloads. Windows 10 If you're an experienced Windows user you'll be pleased with the return of the familiar Start button and menu, while everyone will benefit from the many new and exciting features designed to make accessing what matters to you quick and easy.   Windows 10 features the new Edge browser, which gives you a much bigger viewing area for enjoying your online content at its best. You can write notes directly onto web pages and share them with anyone - perfect for students or business. Working between different software or keeping an eye on social media while you work has never been easier; you can now snap up to four apps to any location on the screen for effortless multitasking. You can even create individual desktops for specific projects and tasks.  Whatever you're doing, Windows 10 makes your PC work the way you want.  For everyday computing on the move, look no further than the Asus E200HA 11.6" Laptop in white.
