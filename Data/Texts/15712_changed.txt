Intel® Core i7™ Processor The  GL72 6QDboasts the latest 6th generation Intel® Core™ i5 processor, consuming less power but delivering even better performance than previous i5 processors. This results in smoother, faster and more enjoyable gameplay as you know the processor will keep up with your demands. Fast gameplay With 8 GB DDR4-2133 memory support, you can enjoy a fast performance that will allow you to immerse yourself in your gaming experience. You can even expand the memory capacity up to a maximum of 16 GB for truly breath-taking gameplay. A 1 TB HDD ensures that there is plenty of storage space on the laptop for your games and files. The GL72 6QD also boasts Cooler Boost technology which boosts the airflow to maintain the laptop and chipsets at a lower temperature to avoid overheating. Plus with a Killer E2400 chipset protecting networking traffic and preventing lag spikes, you can expect fast and smooth gameplay to maximise your enjoyment and immersion. Connectivity The  GL72 6QD features an HDMI port and a Mini Display Port, as well as a USB 3.0 slots and a USB-C slot for plenty of connection options. You can also immerse yourself in your gaming experience by connecting up to three external displays in Full HD with MSI Matrix Display technology. It is even possible to enjoy 4k outputs with screens capable of boasting a resolution up to 3840 x 2160 (sold separately). Immersive experience With the power of the NVIDIA GeForce GTX 950M graphics card, you can enjoy extremely fast and fluid gaming experiences. The high quality sound technology of the  GL72 6QD brings games to life. Nahimic technology offers virtual 7.1 surround sound in immersive high definition. As a result, you can maintain the edge in competitive games by hearing every footstep, every explosion, every bullet whizzing past as you fight for victory. Offering incredible power and the opportunity to experience games in incredible detail, the MSI  GL72 6QD 022UK 17.3" Gaming Laptop  is an ideal choice for the serious gamer. ___________________________________________________________________________ Ultrabook, Celeron, Celeron Inside, Core Inside, Intel, Intel Logo, Intel Atom, Intel Atom Inside, Intel Core, Intel Inside, Intel Inside Logo, Intel vPro, Itanium, Itanium Inside, Pentium, Pentium Inside, vPro Inside, Xeon, Xeon Phi and Xeon Inside are trademarks of Intel Corporation in the U.S. and/or other countries. Next day delivery (from £3.95): on orders placed before 9pm Monday-Friday, or 4pm on weekends. Tell us your mobile number and on the day of the delivery we'll text you to say what time your order will be with you. Same day delivery (£3.95): exclusively for selected addresses in Greater London. Order online and collect in store Sometimes you’ll want something even quicker than we can deliver. Or you can’t be in when our van or courier would arrive. That’s why we offer you the option to order online and collect in store. Subject to what you’re ordering and where you want to collect from, you can: Reserve & Collect – reserve online for free and collect at a local store as soon as you like. Once reserved, your item’s held till closing time the next day, to be paid for when you collect. Pay & Collect – pay online, and have the item sent, for free, from our warehouse to a local store for you to collect. Starting on the date the item arrives in store – which you choose in checkout – you have 28 days to collect it.
