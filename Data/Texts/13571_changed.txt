Durable Mobile Design Rest assured that the HP 250 can keep up with assignments on the run. The durable chassis protects the notebook so it looks as professional as you do. Powered for Business Powered by Intel Celeron N3060 processor, 4GB of RAM, and 128 GB SSD, the HP 250 (model # Y1V08UT#ABA) gives you the power you need to blast through any tasks. Windows 10 Home Enhance all you do with the new Windows 10 Home. Windows 10 Home brings back the Start Menu from Windows 7 and introduces new features, like the Edge Web browser that lets you markup Web pages on your screen. Cloud-based IT Management Made Simple HP Touchpoint Manager with Always On Remote Management [1] offers users simple tools and services from a single cloud-based solution for managing data and security. Help Secure Critical Data The firmware Trusted Platform Module (TPM) [2] provides hardware-based encryption keys to help secure your data, e-mail, and user credentials. Crisp, Clear View Enjoy your entertainment in amazing clarity with the 15.6" LED-backlit HD SVA anti-glare display. Hear it, See it Make the most of virtual conferencing [3]. Bring work to life through immersive surround sound with deep, rich bass and crystal clear dialog using DTS Studio Sound software. Be Your Best Virtual Self Sometimes in life, only a face-to-face conversation will do. Thanks to the HP's HD Webcam, you always come off looking your best in virtual conferences [3], even in low light. Cut out the Chatter Help suppress ambient noise including keyboard clicks with HP Noise Reduction Software. Excellent Entertainment The SuperMulti DVD+/-RW drive allows you to watch DVDs and create backups for your work. Hassle-free Connectivity Get fast connections with Gigabit LAN and WLAN 802.11ac/Bluetooth 4.2 combo [4]. Note: [1]. HP Touchpoint Manager requires purchase of a subscription and supports Android, iOS and Windows 7 or higher operating systems and PCs, notebooks, tablets and smartphones from various manufacturers. Not available in all countries. [2]. Firmware TPM is version 2.0. Hardware TPM is v1.2, which is a subset of the TPM 2.0 specification version v0.89 as implemented by Intel Platform Trust Technology (PTT). [3]. Requires an Internet connection. [4]. Wireless access point and Internet access required. Availability of public wireless access points limited. The specifications for the 802.11ac WLAN are draft specifications and are not final.
