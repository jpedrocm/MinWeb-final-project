They come with a slim, lightweight design that weighs under 4 pounds so they're easy to transport, and plenty of features that make them an excellent personal or business solution. Users have access to all Chrome apps to boost productivity or performance. Staples carries several models and configurations to meet your needs. Impressive Visuals and Graphics for Clarity The HP Chromebook comes in two sizes: 11 and 14 inches. Some screens have the BrightView LED technology with 1366 x 768 resolution while others offer anti-glare IPS with 1920 x 1080 resolution that provides a clear viewing angle no matter what time of day it is. Intel HD Graphics technology improves the appearance of videos and games, and fast processing speeds ensure a quality viewing experience. Intel Processors for Fast Performance All Chromebook models feature the Intel Celeron processor family and up to 4GB of DDR3L SDRAM. These provide users with a platform for fast web browsing, data processing and seamless multitasking. A 16GB eMMC solid state drive provides built-in storage for documents, photos and videos. Those looking for more will appreciate the Google Drive technology that provides 100GB of online space so users can access their data across multiple devices using cloud technology. With 802.11ac wireless and Bluetooth capability, connecting to Wi-Fi and peripherals is easy and helps improve productivity. Users can pair a wireless headset with a built-in microphone for business calls, or use the integrated headphone jack. Built-In Features for Business Productivity With a built-in TrueVision webcam, digital microphone and speakers, HP Chromebooks support video conferencing via Skype or other apps. The multi-touch trackpad comes with gesture support that lets users scroll, flick, pinch, zoom and rotate images and pages on the screen. External ports include HDMI, and multiple USB ports as well as a media card reader to access files stored on flash drives or memory cards. Some models are Energy Star certified to consume less energy while charging. Many offer an extended battery life, with up to 8 hours of power on a single charge, making it an ideal device for traveling.  A full-size island-style keyboard resembles a desktop unit, making it easy to type, and a with a slim profile that measures less than 1 inch thick, it easily fits in a laptop bag or briefcase. Advanced Functions for Improved Security Users looking to protect the contents of their device will benefit from the built-in security measures, including a power-on password. In the management settings, users can create multiple profile settings with individual passwords to prevent crossover or unauthorized access. The Kensington MicroSaver lock slot provides additional security from theft when you add on one of their cable lock systems. The HP Chromebook also works with other third-party lock devices. With built-in versatility and a reliable operating system, Chromebooks are a viable computing solution for a variety of business or personal needs. Staples can help you find a new or refurbished HP Chromebook and a wide variety of accessories to go with them. The Kensington MicroSaver lock slot provides additional security from theft when you add on one of their cable lock systems. The HP Chromebook also works with other third-party lock devices. With built-in versatility and a reliable operating system, Chromebooks are a viable computing solution for a variety of business or personal needs.
