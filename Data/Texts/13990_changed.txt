HP TrueVision HD Webcam Capture all the details with vibrant clarity, even in low light. Enjoy the nuances of face-to-face conversations. Packed with Many Amenities Complete with a rich set of extra features, the HP Pavilion 17-f053us makes life easier. Effortlessly connect to the internet through Ethernet LAN or wireless WLAN Transfer large files in seconds via the SuperSpeed USB 3.0 ports, and say goodbye to long waiting times. Simply hook the laptop up to a big HDTV through its included HDMI port, and take your visual experience to the next level. Energy Efficiency Your Way HP is committed to global citizenship and environmental responsibility.
