Take it with you anywhere, and stay highly productive while on the go. Equipped for Productivity The 3rd generation Intel Core i5 vPro processor delivers 2.60 GHz of number-crunching power to seamlessly handle data-intensive applications. When your workload ramps up, your PC keeps up with added processor performance, thanks to the support for Intel Turbo Boost Technology 2.0 [1] . 4 GB of high-bandwidth memory assures you that multitasking goes smoothly. With up to 120 GB SSD, the storage capacity and system's responsiveness go hand in hand. Truly Built for Business The Intel vPro technology [2] helps reduce IT maintenance costs by enabling remote configuration, diagnosis, isolation and repair of infected PCs. HP's wireless technology[3] has you covered, too. Stay connected with essential ports for additional displays, USB devices and more. Constructed for Business On the Go With a wear-resistant tungsten-colored finish, this mainstream business notebook polishes off your professional look. The 14.0-inch diagonal display provides a crisp view while remaining highly portable. You'll easily carry this notebook to meetings down the hall and around the world. NOTE: [1]. Intel Turbo Boost performance varies depending on hardware, software and overall system configuration. [2]. Some functionality of this technology, such as Intel Active management technology and Intel Virtualization technology, requires additional 3rd party software in order to run. Availability of future "virtual appliances" applications for Intel vPro technology is dependent on 3rd party software providers. Microsoft Windows required. [3]. Wireless use requires separately purchased service contract.
